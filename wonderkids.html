Development Tips

  1. Regular Game Time: Ensure your wonderkids get enough playing time, either in your first team or on loan.
  2. Training Focus: Set specific training focuses based on their position and attributes that need improvement.
  3. Mentoring: Pair them with experienced players who have good personalities and relevant attributes.
  4. Facilities: Invest in top-quality training facilities to maximize their development.
  5. Patience: Some players develop later than others - don't give up on them too soon.

Scouting Tips

  • Focus on countries with strong youth development systems (Brazil, Argentina, France, etc.)
  • Look for players with good personalities (Professional, Determined, etc.)
  • Check their injury history - avoid players with recurring injuries
  • Consider their adaptability to your league and playing style
