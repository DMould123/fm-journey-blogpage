Leeds United: Reviving the Glory Days

Jon Doe May 2025 8 min read

A detailed guide to restoring Leeds United to its former glory in Football Manager.

Suggested Best Formation

4-2-3-1 Wide: This formation utilizes Leeds' work rate and attacking talent, providing width and support for the striker.
Click the image above to view the video guide on YouTube.

Squad Overview

Leeds has a mix of young talent and experienced players. Focus on improving the defense and adding creativity in midfield.

Tactical Recommendations

Employ a high-intensity 4-2-3-1 formation to capitalize on the team's work rate and attacking potential.

Transfer Targets

  • James Ward-Prowse (Central Midfielder): A set-piece specialist and midfield leader who could add much needed quality and experience.
  • Max Aarons (Right Back): A dynamic right-back home-grown option.
  • Adam Hložek (Attacking Midfielder / Striker): A versatile attacking player could add much needed depth.
