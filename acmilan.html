AC Milan: Restoring European Dominance

A guide to bringing AC Milan back to the pinnacle of European football.

Squad Overview

AC Milan has a strong core of players, including Rafael Leão and Sandro Tonali. Focus on adding depth to the squad.

Tactical Recommendations

Use a 4-2-3-1 or 4-3-3 formation to maximize the team's attacking flair and defensive stability.

Transfer Targets

  • Alessandro Bastoni: A reliable center-back option.
  • Dominik Szoboszlai: A creative attacking midfielder.
  • Benjamin Šeško: A promising young striker.
