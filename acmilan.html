AC Milan: Restoring European Dominance

Jon Doe May 2025 8 min read

A guide to bringing AC Milan back to the pinnacle of European football.

Suggested Best Formation

4-2-3-1 Wide:

This formation leverages Milan's attacking flair and defensive stability.

Squad Overview

AC Milan has a strong core of players, including Rafael Leão and Sandro Tonali. Focus on adding depth to the squad.

Tactical Recommendations

Use a 4-2-3-1 or 4-3-3 formation to maximize the team's attacking flair and defensive stability.

Transfer Targets

  • Alessandro Bastoni (Centre Back): A reliable center-back option.
  • Dominik Szoboszlai (Attacking Midfielder): A creative attacking midfielder.
  • Benjamin Šeško (Striker): A promising young striker.
